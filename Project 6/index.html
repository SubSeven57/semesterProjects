Silent Planet

Silent Planet is a progressive metalcore band from Los Angeles, California. The band is signed to Solid State records and to date, they have released two EPS and two records.

Silent Planet's sophomore album, entitled "Everything Was Sound" has been critically acclaimed for its unique blend of athmospheric elements and progressive melodies.
