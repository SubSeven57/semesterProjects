About The Project and Developer

The Project: What is PLACEHOLDER?

I'm glad you asked! PLACEHOLDER is a website dedicated to showing the great parts of the city of Chicago. Fairly often, websites that dedicate themselves to showing off cities only focus of super trendy places. Which is good, but not good enough for Chicago. This city is know for its diversity and that's exactly what I wanted to show off.
